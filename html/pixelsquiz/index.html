Note: Teams are numbered from 1-4 for display purposes, but are 0-3 internally.

Bill of Materials

  • Your laptop and its power adapter;
  • VGA adapter for your laptop
  • Ethernet adapter for your laptop
  • A-B USB cable (short);
  • A-type USB extension cable (long);
  • Powered USB hub and its power supply;
  • Buzzers with their controller;
  • Ethernet switch and its power supply;
  • 5 ethernet cables;
  • 4 Raspberry Pis and their SD cards;
  • 4 USB power supplies (for the Raspberry Pi);
  • 4 HDMI cables;
  • 4 monitors (provided by Hipnose);

Assembly Checklist

  1. Disable your firewall;
  2. Disable standby/blank screen on your laptop;
  3. Turn the ethernet switch on;
  4. Connect your laptop to the network (and optionally disable your wireless connection);
  5. Connect the buzzers to the powered USB hub;
  6. Connect the powered USB hub to your laptop (using the long cable);
  7. Connect the Raspberry Pis to the pulpit monitors;
  8. Connect the Raspberry Pis to the network (ethernet switch);
  9. Connect to the Raspberry Pis via SSH (pi@192.168.114.101 to 104)

Pre-flight Checklist

  1. Remove the previous game state;
  2. Start the game engine: lein run;
  3. Check all four buzzers;
  4. Start the player screens on the four Raspberry Pis (./start_screen.sh over SSH);
  5. Open the quiz console on Safari (other browsers may reject answers when switching tabs)
  6. Open the main screen;
  7. Open your team roster spreadsheet (where winners will be recorded);
  8. ...
  9. Profit!
